How to compress PDF to a specific size?

At ToolFlyer, we provide three levels of optimization. The High Compression mode is ideal for government portals and email attachments where file size limits are strict. Medium is our most popular setting, balancing clear text with a reduced footprint. Low keeps your images in high resolution while stripping away unnecessary metadata.
