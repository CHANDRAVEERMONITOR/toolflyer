How ToolFlyer Extreme Compression Works

Unlike standard tools that only clean metadata, ToolFlyer uses an image-reconstruction engine. We render each page as a high-quality image, apply JPEG compression, and re-bundle it into a PDF. This ensures 100% success in reducing file sizes for portals like jameenseva.in or Bihar Land Records where 200KB limits are common.

1. Is my PDF searchable after this?
Since this method converts pages to images for maximum compression, the text becomes part of the image. For searchable PDFs, use our "Standard Compression" mode.
2. Why does it take time?
Our tool processes everything in your browser to ensure 100% privacy. It doesn't upload your file to any server. This takes a bit of your device's RAM to process pages one by one.
3. Is it safe for Google Search Console?
Yes! Our pages are optimized for fast loading and meet all Core Web Vitals, making them perfect for ranking on Google.
